Tunisian Couscous

Description

Tunisian Couscous is a flavorful and hearty dish made with spiced semolina, vegetables, and your choice of protein. It’s a staple of Tunisian cuisine and perfect for a family feast.

By Allrecipes Member

Tunisian Couscous Ingredients

  • 2 cups couscous
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon harissa paste
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 pound lamb or chicken, cubed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

* Step I

Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened. Add harissa paste and stir well.

* Step II

Add the meat and cook until browned. Stir in stock, zucchini, and carrot. Simmer for 25-30 minutes until the vegetables and meat are tender.

* Step III

Prepare couscous according to the package instructions. Serve the cooked couscous topped with the meat and vegetable mixture.
