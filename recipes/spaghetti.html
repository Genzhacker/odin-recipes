Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is perfect for a hearty family dinner. It’s easy to make and features a rich, savory tomato sauce with your favorite seasonings.


By Allrecipes Member

Spaghetti Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

Step 1

Boil the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and sauté until softened. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, seasoning with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce. Serve warm, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
